Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is the most popular lasagna in the entirety of allrecipes.com. It was created over twenty years ago by John Chandler

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spcies and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
